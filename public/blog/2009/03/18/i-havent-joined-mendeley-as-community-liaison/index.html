I haven't joined Mendeley as community liaison

Mar 18th, 2009

Despite rumors floating in the upper echelons on the interwebs, I must admit that I haven’t been approached, contacted, persuaded or forced to join Mendeley as a Community Liaison. I understand the rumours were founded on the statement from one of the company’s staff member that my role in the software development is “crucial” (not his exact words, but in the ballpark).

I wish Gunn and Vidal all the luck.
