Image via WikipediaThe contents of this site/blog are being (slowly) transferred to a wiki-like page on Open Wet Ware. Thanks to Ricardo Vidal that started including the articles and was the person responsible for this initiative (and a great artist, illustrator). What is the OWW? OpenWetWare is an effort to promote the sharing of information, know-how, and wisdom among researchers and groups who are working in biology & biological engineering. Learn more about us. If you would like edit access, would be interested in helping out, or want your lab website hosted on OpenWetWare, please join us.
